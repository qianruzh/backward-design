Skills for Making Coffee

Here are instructions on how to make coffee.

Ice Americano

  • Pull 2 espresso shots from the Breville Barista Expresso Espresso Maker with freshly ground beans.
  • Pour over a cup of ice filled to the rim.
  • Add chilled filtered water and enjoy.

Ice latte

  • Fill a cup with cold milk.
  • Brew one shot of espresso in an espresso machine like the Nespresso Vertuoline Evoluo Deluxe, pour into the cup, and stir.
  • Add ice and serve.
