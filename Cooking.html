Skills for Cooking

Here are some skills that you should know before cooking.

Cutting foods.

You can cut like a professional chef using a knife to slice herbs, dice onions or cut meat.

  • The handle grip where your thumb and forefinger are on the handle of the knife.
  • The blade grip where the thumb and forefinger hold the top of the blade of the knife.

Cover the pot timely.

The use of a lid seems to depend on what is being cooked, and how long it is being cooked.

  • If you're steaming green vegetables, and they will be steamed for more than five minutes, then you should not use the lid.
  • If you're boiling water to cook pasta, which requires a large amount of water, a lid is good to use at first.
