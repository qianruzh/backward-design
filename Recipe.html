Pick up a recipe

Here are some recipies for different coffee.

Americano.

Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, traditionally brewed coffee. The strength of an Americano varies with the number of shots of espresso and the amount of water added.

You can make an Americano by:

  • Pull 2 espresso shots from the Breville Barista Expresso Espresso Maker with freshly ground beans.
  • Pour over a cup of ice filled to the rim.
  • Add chilled filtered water and enjoy.

Latte.

Latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. There are variants such as the chocolate-flavored mocha or replacing the coffee with another drink base such as masala chai (spiced Indian tea), mate, matcha, turmeric or rooibos; other types of milk, such as soy milk or almond milk, are also used.

You can make an Latte by:

  • Fill a cup with cold milk.
  • Brew one shot of espresso in an espresso machine like the Nespresso Vertuoline Evoluo Deluxe, pour into the cup, and stir.
  • Add ice and serve.
